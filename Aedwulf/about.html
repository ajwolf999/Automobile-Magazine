About us

Aedwulf automagazine is an Polish automobile magazine published by the Aedwulf Magazine Group. It was founded by Abhijit Sengupta in 2019. The aim of Aedwulf magazine is to provide latest information about supercars from various brands like: buggati,lamborghini & koeingsegg etc.
